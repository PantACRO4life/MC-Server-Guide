*************
Chunk Loader Guide
*************

_______________

**Chunk loaders are working like this:**

_______________

**[Online Loader]**

Place an [Iron block] in the world and click on it with a [Blaze Rod] the maximum value of Chunks loaded is 49 or 1 area of 7x7. You can see the chunks area with [F9 key]

_______________

**[Offline Loader]**

Place a [Diamond block] in the world and click on it with a [Blaze Rod] the maximum value of Chunks loaded is 9 or 1 area of 3x3. You can see the chunk area with [F9 key]
